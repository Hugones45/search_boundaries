First of all, thank you so much for trying out City Boundaries!

Created for those who love technology and geography, this app offers a fun way to discover the world by exploring the boundaries that shape our civilizations.

Currently in its testing phase, City Boundaries uses three APIs — ArcGIS, Geopify, and Weather Time — to deliver the best possible experience.

Simply type the name of any city, state, or country to jump straight to that location on the map. When searching for cities, you'll also see additional information about the local weather.

You can also change the basemap to improve the visualization of your search.

If your search fails, it may be due to reaching the API request limit. At the moment, the app uses only free APIs, which have relatively low call limits.
